This website outlines one of my favorite past-times and passions - singing.

From a young age, I liked to lipsync and sing songs and I would often put on 'Saturday Morning Concerts' for my parents as they drank their morning coffee in the living room.

I would dress up and sometimes paint my face for these 'concerts.' I performed in my mother's fancy shoes and jewelry, or my dad's leather jacket with one of his ties around my forehead, and sometimes I would dust off the old Halloween costumes, like my ladybug, and perform entire CDs worth of songs.

At the age of 11, I started teaching myself classical guitar, and received my first electric guitar for Christmas at 14. From there, I would enjoy playing inn jam sessions in the basement with my friends, and was part of a group of kids in school that also enjoyed playing and performing music. I got my first acoustic guitar at 16 and then started writing comical songs and enjoyed playing for friends and family.

Later in life, I have joined various choirs and was a singer in a 11-piece funk band, and even secured positions with a local musical theatre group. I have always loved singing and try to continue doing as much as I can.

Use the navigation tools to jump to sections of my career and experience in singing and I hope you enjoy some of the performance videos.

Cheers x
